### FAQ

**Q: How accurate is your carbon data?**

A: We don't claim a single accuracy percentage because it varies by material. Instead, we show confidence scores (High/Medium/Low) on every match. High confidence matches (typically 85-95% name similarity + category match) are likely accurate. Low confidence matches should be verified independently. Every report includes a data quality summary so you know what to trust.

**Q: Can I use this for LEED/BREEAM submissions?**

A: You can use our analysis as a starting point, but certification bodies typically require independent verification. We provide the data quality documentation and verification guides to make that process easier. Think of us as a guidance tool, not a replacement for professional carbon consultants.

**Q: Why do you show "low confidence" matches?**

A: Because hiding uncertainty would be dishonest. A low confidence match with a clear warning is better than a false sense of certainty. You deserve to know when to verify - that's professional-grade transparency.

**Q: How often is your EPD data updated?**

A: Our database is updated quarterly. We show EPD publication dates on every material and flag data >2 years old. For critical materials, we recommend checking manufacturer websites for newer EPDs.

**Q: What if I find an error in your data?**

A: Please report it! We have a "Report Issue" button in the plugin and actively welcome corrections. Honesty means admitting and fixing mistakes quickly.

**Q: Do you guarantee regulatory compliance?**

A: No - and be wary of any tool that does. Carbon regulations vary by jurisdiction and change frequently. We provide methodology documentation and verification guides, but ultimate compliance responsibility rests with you and your consultants.
